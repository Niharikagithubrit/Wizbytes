About Summit

A Panel Discussion Will Include A More Comprehensive View Of Defi, Future Market Structures, And How The Old World Will Transition To Modern Reality. Defi Is A Catch-all Word For A Vision Of A Financial Economy Free Of Middlemen. Decentralized Finance (DeFi) Refers To An Ecosystem Of Financial Applications That Are Built On Top Of Blockchain Networks. It Is Based On Shared Blockchains Such As Bitcoin, Ethereum, And Other Cryptocurrencies.

We'll Use Our Partners' Insights To Address Some Of The More Important Questions About How Emerging Business Models Challenge The Existing Financial System, What New Governance Mechanisms Are Needed, And What It Means For The Average Person. This Global DeFi Summit 2021 will be the largest dedicated conference & event focused specifically on Decentralized Finance.
